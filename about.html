About Student Portal

A comprehensive academic management and data visualization system.

Our Vision

The Student Portal is designed to provide a transparent and data-driven approach to educational management. By centralizing student performance metrics, we empower educators to identify trends, support low-performing individuals, and celebrate high achievers in real-time.

Data Accuracy

No machine learning models are used. All analytics are based on actual verified marks and attendance data.

Secure Access

Institutional data is protected via secure session handling and encrypted password storage.

Core Functions

  • Batch Submission: Simplified mark entry for multiple subjects in one go.
  • Real-time Analytics: Instant visualization of class distribution and subject averages.
  • Leaderboards: Automatic ranking of students based on academic performance.
  • Report Generation: One-click CSV export for offline records.

Need Support?

For administrative issues or bug reports, please contact the IT department at support@studentportal.edu
